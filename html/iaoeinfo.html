What is IAoE?

The Institute of Age of Empires (IAoE) is a hobby project of mine that I, Batuhan ÖZ, code along to my WebDev journey.
I aim to mix my passion for Age of Empires and my interest in Web Development by creating engaging content and tools for fans of the game.

The IAoE is a platform where players can find information about the game, including guides, strategies, and resources to enhance their gameplay experience.
The website is designed to be user-friendly and accessible, making it easy for players of all skill levels to navigate and find the information they need.
I aim to create tools that will help players improve their skills and knowledge of the game, such as calculators for unit stats, build orders, and other useful resources.
Gain the bleeding-edge in-game knowledge by being a student of IAoE. Improve your gameplay today!
